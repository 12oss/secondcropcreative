Gender Inequality in Africa

Poster Design - UW-Stout

Gender Inequality in Africa Poster Design

The objective of this Graphic Design I project at UW-Stout was to create a design based around a social issue in a specific geogrpahical location in the world. THe task started by students drawing both a social issue and geographical location randomly from a hat. After that students researched their social issue and began the design process. The end goal, make a design that shed light on said social issue in the geographical location chosen in a visual manner.
