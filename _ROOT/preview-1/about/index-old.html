CAREER HISTORY

Pamela launched her graphic design career with the exhibit design firm Spagnola & Associates‚ then went on to work in the publishing industry. During this time she gained experience with photo shoot production‚ budgeting‚ and directing staff.

After working in editorial design and freelancing at night designing websites‚ Pamela accepted a position as Design Director at SuperNova Productions Inc. SuperNova is a full-service digital media agency specializing in creative technological solutions that help companies build their business‚ communicate their vision‚ and increase customer loyalty. As a hands-on creative lead‚ her responsibilities included designing‚ directing‚ managing time and budgets‚ and working closely with all cross-functional partners to ensure a steady work flow and consistent quality for the company.

Pamela was approached by AIM Media in 2004‚ and relocated to Virginia for a year to work closely with the publisher and general manager within AIM’s Homebuyers Division. AIM Media was seeking to acquire and build market-leading enthusiast publishing‚ direct marketing‚ and event-management organizations serving the niche consumer growth markets of art‚ home‚ healthy living‚ and enthusiast sports. In addition to building up the creative department within the appointed division‚ she had the opportunity to assist with strategic planning and new business development.

Pamela launched an independent freelance design business in 2005‚ accepting design consultant opportunities with former employers and several design firms. The majority of her work as a design consultant included brand identity‚ concept design and digital exploration.

Lands’ End recruited Pamela in 2008‚ and she accepted a position as Digital Senior Art Director for the Kids’ Division and relocated to the Midwest. In her current role‚ she is responsible for executing all digital creative for landsend.com‚ including creating unique experiences and concepting for marketing e-mails‚ affiliates‚ social media‚ special campaigns‚ and contests. In addition‚ special projects gives Pamela the opportunity to utilize skills in story boarding‚ directing video in-house and on location‚ and directing still-life photography. Her role encompasses working closely with cross-functional teams in an omni-channel environment and mentoring junior staff and interns.

Pamela graduated from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art with a BFA in graphic design and typography‚ and did an extension to her studies abroad at Middlesex Polytechnic University in London. After working in the field for several years exploring many avenues of design she went on to earn an MA in media studies and media management from The New School University.
