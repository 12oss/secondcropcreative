Kids’ outerwear experience at landsend.com

This was a cross-functional effort to highlight the Squall® outerwear collection for kids. This experience included two videos‚ details of outerwear features‚ and animated slides. All video concepts were lead by the group creative director with input from the design team. The Squallhouse video was outsourced to an agency and the Squall-has-it-all video was produced in-house. The custom font used was drawn by an in-house illustrator and used for a seamless omni-channel experience throughout the season.

Tasks included:

  • art direction and design execution
  • initial video concepts
  • design for animated details
  • digital presentation
