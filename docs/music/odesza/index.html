Odesza - Painting Soundscapes at The Majestic

BY ROSS HARRIED

Painting Soundscapes at The Majestic

Comprised of Harrison Mills and Clayton Knight, the duo's journey began in 2012, just as they were graduating from Western Washington University. Their debut album, "Summer's Gone," released the same year, quickly gained recognition within the underground electronic music scene.

Following the success of their debut EP, "My Friends Never Die," Odesza released their sophomore album, "In Return," on September 9, 2014, marking their debut on Ninja Tune and Counter Records. This performance at The Majestic showcased tracks from "In Return" alongside earlier releases, captivating the audience with their signature blend of ambient textures and pulsating rhythms. From Bellingham to Madison, Odesza's music transcends boundaries, enveloping listeners in an electro odyssey of pure euphoria.
