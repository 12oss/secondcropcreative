Midwest Funk Poster Design

The Midwest Funk poster design was a case study I did for The Majestic Theater in Madison, WI. I've done a ton of freelance photography for The Majestic, and they had a graphic design position open up a few years back. I threw my hat into the ring and part of the interview process to create a kickass poster for a mock event. This was my end result that gained me an interview and further discussions.
