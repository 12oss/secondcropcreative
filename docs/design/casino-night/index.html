Casino Night Poster Design

Another day at my graphic design job at UW-Stout, another poster design. I am particulaly proud of the colors nad textures in the Casino Night poster. One of the struggles I had as a designer for UW-Stout was keeping things fresh and new year after year, or season after season as most of the programs and events where repeated. Casino Night was one of those events - I probably made 3 or 4 of those posters, but this is my favorite one of the bunch.
