Black Diamond Bay Poster Design

Blue Devil Productions is UW-Stout's premiere entertainment organizer. They gather and put on shows ranging from movies, to comedians, to rock shows. One of the most sought after jobs was the graphic design position within BDP. My talented friend and colleague had already owned that coveted spot, but every once and a while he would need help with a poster or two. I helped him out with the Black Diamond Bay poster, but with no real direction. So I took a bunch of found objects and photos and mashed them together in this masterpiece.
