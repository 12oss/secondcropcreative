David Robinson, or as I like to remember him, Tim Duncan's older wiser great uncle. Robinson was an impressive beast on the court, but furthermore, does anyone remember how ripped this guy was. At 7'1", I don't think David had a lick of body fat on him; a complete change of pace compared to Shaq.
