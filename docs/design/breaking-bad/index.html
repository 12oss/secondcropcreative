Breaking Bad Watercolor Painting

Walter White & Jessie Pinkman - two unlikely business partners, brought to to life via watercolor on canvas. Breaking Bad is probably in my top 5 shows of all time, and in an effort to continue with my digital waterolor techniques, I thought I would spend an afternoon playing around the the Blue Ice kingpins of New Mexico.
