Jonathan Quick Watercolor

This Jonathan Quick L.A. Kings watercolor was done as a Christmas gift for a friend of my mother. It just turns out, my mother is not only my biggest client, but also my biggest referral. Anywho, I was given the task to do an 'L.A. Kings watercolor thing for Christmas.' I did some research and finally settled on Jonathan Quick, as the goalie (in my uneducated opinion) is one of, if not the most important player on the ice.
