For my birthday, my wife gifted me two tickets (and a parking pass) to go see Judah And The Lion at The Rave in Milwaukee, WI. For all of you who do not know, I am a former concert photographer, and have a great appreciation for music. So, given the opportunity I 'snuck' a small point and shoot into the show (air quotes, because I didn't really hide the fact that I had a camera with me). I originally just want to test out how my little Canon G7X would handle the harsh conditions of concert photography - and it held up ok, you know, for casual snaps. However, after a while, I figured I would try and grab some video too, becasue hell, all the Millenials in the room had their cell phones raised to the ceiling trying to capture shitty quality snapchats and what have you.

Below you will find all the snaps and video I kept from the concert and I have to mention a few things. First off, thanks to the awesome staff at The Rave, that never once challenged me to put the camera down. Next - guys, if you've not seen Judah And The Lion in concert, do yourself a solid and go check them out. I have determined they are probably somewhere in the top three live performances in recent memory (sharing that coveted spot with 21 Pilots & Matt & Kim). And finally, I highly encourage every to consider looking into the Canon G7X if you just want to dip your toes into the casual photographers waters. It's an awesome little camera, and quickly becoming my go to pocketable travel/concert camera.
