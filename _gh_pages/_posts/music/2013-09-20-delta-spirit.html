The night was filled with soulful melodies and energetic vibes, complemented by the talents of openers Phox and Hugh Bob & The Hustle.

Hugh Bob & The Hustle kicked off the evening with their Americana-infused sound, setting the stage for an unforgettable night of music. Following their spirited performance, Phox took the stage with their enchanting blend of indie pop and folk-rock, captivating the crowd with their ethereal vocals and infectious rhythms.

As the night progressed, anticipation mounted for the headlining act, Delta Spirit. With their dynamic range and musical prowess, Delta Spirit took the stage by storm, delivering a powerhouse performance that had the crowd hanging on every note. From anthemic rockers to heartfelt ballads, Delta Spirit's set was a testament to the band's undeniable talent and stage presence.
