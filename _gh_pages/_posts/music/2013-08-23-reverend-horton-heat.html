The night was a raucous celebration of music and community, with fans coming together to revel in the infectious energy of live performance.

Kicking off the evening, White Iron Band set the stage on fire with their high-octane blend of blues, rock, and country. Their gritty vocals and fiery instrumentals got the crowd pumped up and ready for a night of rock 'n' roll revelry.

As Reverend Horton Heat took the stage, the atmosphere crackled with anticipation. With their signature blend of rockabilly, punk, and surf rock, they delivered a blistering performance that had the crowd dancing and cheering from start to finish. Hits like "Psychobilly Freakout" and "Galaxy 500" had everyone on their feet, lost in the music.

Together, Reverend Horton Heat and White Iron Band delivered a night of music that was nothing short of electrifying. From the blistering guitar solos to the thunderous drum beats, it was a celebration of all things rock 'n' roll that left a lasting impression on everyone lucky enough to be in attendance.
