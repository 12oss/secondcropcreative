The night was filled with high-energy performances, killer riffs, and memorable moments that left the audience wanting more.

Sick Puppies, known for their electrifying live shows, didn't disappoint. With their powerhouse vocals and adrenaline-fueled instrumentals, they had the crowd on their feet from the moment they stepped on stage. The female bassist of Sick Puppies delivered killer shots that captured the essence of the band's performance, while the bassist of Devour the Day also impressed with his stage presence and energy.

Devour the Day, the opening act, set the tone for the night with their hard-hitting rock anthems and infectious energy. Their dynamic performance warmed up the crowd and got everyone ready for an unforgettable evening of music.
