With balloons, confetti, crowd surfing and an infectious energy that swept through the crowd, Matt Johnson and Kim Schifino transformed the venue into a frenzy of ecstatic fans.

Formed in 2004, Matt and Kim are renowned for their upbeat dance music and energetic live performances. Comprising Matt Johnson on vocals and keyboards, and Kim Schifino on drums, the duo's chemistry onstage is undeniable. From intimate loft shows to headlining slots at major festivals like Bonnaroo and Coachella, Matt and Kim's journey has been defined by their infectious sound and unbridled enthusiasm.

With six studio albums under their belt, including the gold-certified "Grand" featuring the hit single "Daylight," Matt and Kim have solidified their status as indie electronic icons. Their willingness to push boundaries was evident in the music video for "Lessons Learned," which won the Breakthrough Video Award at the 2009 MTV Video Music Awards. At The Majestic, Matt and Kim unleashed their trademark energy, leaving an indelible mark on Madison and reaffirming their reputation as one of the most dynamic acts in the music scene.
