The night was a whirlwind of infectious beats, vibrant melodies, and unforgettable moments.

Kicking off the evening, Probcause set the tone with his eclectic blend of hip-hop and electronic elements, warming up the crowd with his energetic performance and clever lyricism. His dynamic stage presence and infectious charisma got everyone grooving and set the stage for the headlining act.

As Cherub took the stage, the energy in the room soared to new heights. With their signature blend of electro-pop and funk-infused beats, they had the audience dancing and singing along from start to finish. Hits like "Doses & Mimosas" and "Chocolate Strawberries" had the crowd in a frenzy, creating an atmosphere of pure euphoria.

Together, Cherub and Probcause delivered a night of music that was nothing short of electrifying. From the infectious rhythms to the pulsating bass lines, it was a celebration of sound and energy that left a lasting impression on everyone in attendance.
