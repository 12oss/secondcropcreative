With the infectious energy they're known for, the band promised an unforgettable experience for fans. Joining them on this exhilarating journey were The Griswolds, ensuring that the tour would be nothing short of epic.

On March 29th, 2015, Madison, WI was treated to a night of pure musical magic as Walk The Moon took the stage. The band's electrifying performance captivated the audience from the first note to the last, delivering a show that was nothing short of spectacular. As a testament to their talent and stage presence, they provided me with unparalleled opportunities, resulting in some of the most stunning shots I've taken to date.

With their infectious beats and undeniable charm, Walk The Moon proved once again why they're a force to be reckoned with in the music industry. Their performance in Madison was a true highlight of the tour, leaving fans eagerly anticipating what the band has in store next.
