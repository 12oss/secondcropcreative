Kicking off the night, Allen Stone mesmerized the audience with his soulful voice and dynamic stage presence. His impassioned performance set the perfect tone for the evening, warming up the crowd and setting the stage for the headlining act to come.

As Andrew McMahon took the stage, the atmosphere was electric with anticipation. With his soaring vocals and masterful piano skills, he delivered a spellbinding performance that left the audience in awe. Hits like "Cecilia and the Satellite" and "Dark Blue" had everyone singing along and swaying to the music, creating a sense of unity and joy.
