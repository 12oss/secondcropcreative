Embarking on a journey across the United States in support of their album of the same name, Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney brought their signature blend of heartfelt lyrics and infectious melodies to Madison as one of their ten tour stops.

Before forming the duo, Shay Mooney was a solo artist on T-Pain's Nappy Boy Entertainment label, while Dan Smyers was involved in various musical projects. Fate brought them together at a party in Nashville in December 2012, where they performed together for the first time and began writing songs immediately. Their debut single, "19 You + Me," released in October 2013, garnered critical acclaim and set the stage for their rapid rise in the country music scene.

Their debut album, "Where It All Began," released in April 2014, further solidified their place in the industry, with singles like "Show You Off" captivating audiences nationwide. With appearances on The Ellen DeGeneres Show and a Grand Ole Opry debut, Dan + Shay's star continued to rise, culminating in a nomination for Vocal Duo of the Year at the 2014 Academy of Country Music Awards. At The Majestic, they delivered a performance that showcased their undeniable talent and cemented their status as one of country music's most promising acts.
