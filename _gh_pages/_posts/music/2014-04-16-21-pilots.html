Opening for them was NONONO, setting the stage on fire for what was to come.

21 Pilots didn't hold back, delivering a performance that was as intense as it was memorable. Fresh from their MTV Movie Awards gig, they brought that same high-octane energy to Madison, captivating the audience with their genre-blurring sound and infectious beats.

From fan favorites like "Car Radio" to new tracks that had the crowd on their feet, 21 Pilots kept the momentum going all night long. Frontmen Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun commanded the stage with their dynamic presence, leaving no doubt that they were born to perform.

As the final chords rang out and the crowd roared for more, it was clear that 21 Pilots had left their mark on The Majestic. It was a night of adrenaline-fueled excitement and pure musical magic that would be remembered long after the last note had faded away.
