Kicking off the evening, Swear & Shake set the stage with their heartfelt lyrics and infectious folk-pop sound. Their dynamic performance warmed up the crowd and set the perfect tone for the headlining act to come.

As ZZ Ward took the stage, the atmosphere crackled with anticipation. With her powerhouse vocals and blues-infused melodies, she captivated the audience from the first note. Hits like "Put The Gun Down" and "365 Days" had the crowd dancing and singing along, creating an electric energy that filled the venue.
